The Search Engine Lucene was used in the context of the postgraduate thesis of Prokopios Stamelias,student in
Departure of Informatics and Telecommunications of University of Athens,under the surveillance of Alexandros
Ntoulas,professor in the aforementioned department.The scope of the work is collecting data from youtube and
tik tok and afterwards the projection of these data in Lucene.Thus,our goal is to collect the data for each user
that used the search engine.
